Instructions



This quiz consists of 30 questions that will test your knowledge of Navy and Marine Corps platforms.


You will be shown a picture of a platform, and you will have 7 seconds to submit the correct answer.


If you get the question correct, you will receive 1 point. If you get a question wrong or don't answer in time, you will lose 3 points.


Do not REFRESH or use the BACK button during the quiz. It may break the quiz and mess up your score.
